How do I play the game?

Color Match Game tests your reflexes and color recognition skills. Read the question at the top - it will ask you to match either the COLOR of the text or the WORD that is shown. Select the correct answer from the options below before time runs out!

What are the difficulty levels?

Choose from three difficulty levels:

  • Easy: 4 options, more time per question
  • Medium: 5 options, balanced gameplay
  • Hard: 6 options, fast-paced challenge

The difficulty also increases progressively as your score gets higher!

  • üèÜ First Victory (50+) - Score 50 or more in a single game
  • üíØ Century (100+) - Score 100 or more in a single game
  • üéØ Master (150+) - Score 150 or more in a single game
  • üìÖ Dedicated Player - Play 10 or more games
  • üåü Veteran - Play 50 or more games

What is the Daily Challenge?

Every day, you get a new target score to reach. Complete the daily challenge to earn rewards and keep your streak going! The challenge resets daily with a new random target.

How do I view my statistics?

From the main menu, tap the "View Detailed Stats" button to see:

  • Your all-time high score
  • Total games played
  • Average score
  • All unlocked achievements

How is scoring calculated?

You earn points for each correct answer. Build up combos (consecutive correct answers) to earn bonus points! Every 5 correct answers in a row gives you an extra point. Wrong answers break your combo and deduct 1 point.
