T or F Game - Support

About T or F Game

T or F Game is an educational trivia game that tests your knowledge across various topics. Answer True or False questions and challenge yourself to see how many correct answers you can get!

Features

  • Wide variety of trivia questions across multiple categories
  • Simple True or False gameplay
  • Track your score and progress
  • Clean and intuitive interface
  • Available on iOS and Android

How to Play

1. Read the question presented on screen

2. Decide whether the statement is True or False

3. Tap the "TRUE" (green) or "FALSE" (red) button to submit your answer

4. Get instant feedback on whether you answered correctly

5. Continue to the next question and improve your score

6. Challenge yourself to get as many correct answers as possible!

Troubleshooting

Game won't start

Try closing and reopening the app. Make sure you have the latest version installed.

Questions not loading

Ensure you have a stable internet connection. The app may need to download questions on first launch.

Buttons not responding

Make sure you're tapping clearly on the TRUE or FALSE buttons. If the issue persists, try restarting the app.

App crashes or freezes

Please restart the app. If the problem persists, try reinstalling the application.

Privacy

T or F Game does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The game runs entirely on your device and does not require an internet connection. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.
