About Maze Game - Race vs AI

Maze Game - Race vs AI is a simple and fun maze puzzle game where players navigate through randomly generated mazes. The goal is to reach the red exit by dragging your way through the maze from the green starting point.

Features

  • Randomly generated mazes for endless gameplay
  • Simple drag-based controls
  • Clean and intuitive interface
  • Available on iOS, Android, and Web

How to Play

1. Find the green cell - this is your starting position

2. Find the red cell - this is your goal

3. Drag on the screen to move your player through the maze

4. Navigate carefully - you can only move where there are no walls

5. Reach the red exit to complete the level

6. Tap New Game to generate a new puzzle

Troubleshooting

Game won't start

Try closing and reopening the app. Make sure you have the latest version installed.

Controls not responding

Ensure you're dragging on the maze area. The drag gesture requires a clear swipe motion in one of the four directions (up, down, left, right).

App crashes or freezes

Please restart the app. If the problem persists, try reinstalling the application.
